How to Play

The Matching Game is a memory-testing game where players need to match pairs of hidden images by flipping them two at a time. To play:
  1. Click on the "Start Game" button to reveal the hidden images for 3 seconds.
  2. After the images are hidden again, flip two tiles at a time by clicking on them.
  3. If the images match, they remain flipped open; if not, they flip back over.
  4. The game continues until all pairs are matched. Try to finish within one minute to win!
